Zuppa Toscana

A Delicious bowl of Zuppa Toscana, yum!

Now I bet you're excited to make this dish, so let's get started!

Description

Zuppa Toscana is a popular italian dish that many people eat at Olive Garden. This is a hearty dish of potatoes, italian sausage, kale and most importantly, Bacon!

Ingredients

  • 3-4 potatoes
  • A package of mild Italian sausage
  • A bushel of Kale chopped up to your likeness
  • Heavy cream (Omit this if you want to cut on calories and fat!)
  • 2 12oz containers of chicken broth
